Professional String Instrument Repair

Since 1962 Janis Music Co. has been providing quality professional repairs on any and all string instruments. From European and Latin American Ethnic instruments to the latest locking -floating trem Electrics, Acoustics and Bluegrass Instruments, we do it all. We provide honest and professional guitar repair and custom modifications for electric, acoustic, bass guitars and other stringed instruments. With over thirty-five years of experience, our quality is unparalleled, and our work proves it. We provide services as simple as a string changes to minor and major setups, neck adjustments, fret work, refrets, peghead, bridge and crack repair; saddle, nut, and pickup installation, just to name a few. I can discuss the cost and options with you at the time of your request. Please feel free to ask me any questions regarding your instrument.
