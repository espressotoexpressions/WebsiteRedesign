Instrument Rental Program

Here Is How It Works

  • You may return the instrument at any time. You are never obligated to buy the instrument

  • We do not charge a security deposit.

  • We do not require a major credit card or do a credit check.

  • We guarantee that our instruments will pass inspection by the music teacher.

  • We do not require a Social Security number.

  • Theft and maintenance coverage is included in these prices. Loaners are available at no charge.

  • You may apply 9 months rent toward the purchase of the instrument your renting or a step up. Optional purchase plan: 12 months, same as cash! 0% finance charge. The first two months payment is required at time of rental.
